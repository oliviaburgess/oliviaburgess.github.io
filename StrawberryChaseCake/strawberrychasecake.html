About

Strawberry ChaseCake is the product of a 7-week long project during the first year of computer science engineering studies at KTH Flemingsberg. The game is coded in C with use of several SDL libraries such as SDL, SDL_image and SDL_net. It's a network based game where 4 users can connect to a host and play together. One player will try to catch the others as they try to collect as many strawberry cakes to win. If the chaser has caught them all, they win. If a collector has gathered enough points, they win.
